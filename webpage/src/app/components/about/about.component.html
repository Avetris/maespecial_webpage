Quantvm Fate is a Solo Game Studio founded in 2021 and based in Spain. Aitor Velez is the generalist game developer behind Quantvm Fate. I come from a background in software development. My goal is to build competence in all technical / programming branches of videogame development. I love the impact that videogames are making in society and the importance that are taking in which refers to digital creation. While I intend to remain a solo developer, I am bringing in collaborators to make the work more fun and to complement my weaker areas. I usually take artistic part of my videogames from free & paid assets founded on the net, although I sometimes try to do them on my own.
